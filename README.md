# Math Jungle 🦁🌴🐘🐒🐆

Welcome to **Math Jungle** – a fun, educational app designed to help kids explore math through the world of jungle animals! This app covers basic arithmetic operations: addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. 

## Overview

The goal of the app is to make math fun, accessible, and engaging for young learners, blending the excitement of a jungle adventure with essential math skills.

## Features
- **Interactive Questions:** Kids can answer questions by typing their answer or selecting options, with immediate feedback.
- **Score Tracking:** Players earn points as they answer correctly, encouraging progress and motivating them to keep learning.
